Fall Detection Algorithms

YOLOv5 / YOLOv8 + Aspect Ratio

Detects humans using YOLO and flags falls when height-to-width ratio drops.

MediaPipe Pose + Angle Analysis

Estimates body landmarks and computes posture angles to detect falls.

MediaPipe Pose + Angle Analysis

Purpose: Detect posture using keypoint estimation to identify falls.

How It Works:

  • MediaPipe detects body landmarks (shoulders, hips, knees).
  • Angles between joints are calculated (e.g. torso-hip, hip-knee).
  • If body is horizontal or angles are too shallow, fall is flagged.

Benefits:

  • Fast, real-time & lightweight (ideal for mobile/edge devices).
  • No need for training or large models.

YOLO + Aspect Ratio

Purpose: Use object detection to detect people and determine posture.

How It Works:

  • YOLOv5/8 detects people in video frames.
  • Aspect ratio (height/width) of bounding box is monitored.
  • Fall is flagged if ratio drops (e.g. below 0.75).

Benefits:

  • High-speed and accurate object detection.
  • Can be combined with tracking or SAR logic.
